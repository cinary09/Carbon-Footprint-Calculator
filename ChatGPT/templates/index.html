Track everyday climate-positive actions

Climate change happens when greenhouse gases trap extra heat in the atmosphere. That leads to rising temperatures, stronger storms, droughts, wildfires, and threats to food, water, and ecosystems around the world.

It is a serious global issue because it affects people, wildlife, coastlines, agriculture, and public health. The good news is that everyday choices can help reduce emissions and build better habits.

What you can do

  • Recycle waste and reduce landfill pollution.
  • Use public transportation, ride a bicycle, or walk more often.
  • Save electricity and water to reduce resource use.
  • Plant trees and support greener environments.
Start tracking

Why tracking helps

Small actions become easier to repeat when they are visible. Logging your progress helps turn good intentions into consistent habits.

How the score works

Each activity gives points based on its impact. The more actions you record, the more your total contribution grows.
